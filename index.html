Meet Lila

Hi! I'm Lila - your lifelong interactive learning assistant. My mission is to help you learn better!

In the following course we will go through the basics of Machine Learning, which form the foundation of Deep Learning.

I will show you videos and ask you questions to check and improve your understanding of the material. For this course, you only need to have taken introductory levels of calculus and linear algebra.

At any time, if you get stuck you can ask me questions and I will do my best to give you a helpful nudge in the right direction.

When you are ready, register above to get started!

Powered by Artificial Intelligence

  • Lila is an intelligent tutor powered by state-of-the-art Artificial Intelligence. Years of natural language processing research combined with the recent resurgence of Deep Learning have finally made it possible to make a system capable of tutoring students in many different of subjects!

  • A growing amount of scientific research is continues to demonstrate that one-on-one, personalized tutoring increases understanding in a way reading and watching video lectures simply cannot. Lila is part of a larger effort to bring interactive, personalized tutoring to everyone; democratizing learning and allowing the education system to meet the growing demands of the modern world.

Personalized, Interactive Learning

No two learners are the same, which is why Lila was built to adapt to your personal learning style and knowledge. The more Lila knows about how you learn, the better Lila will be able to show you relevant content. The more Lila knows about your knowledge and understanding, the quicker Lila will be able hone in on exercises and feedback important for you.

Continuous Improvement

The power of Artificial Intelligence and Deep Learning allows Lila to continue improve every day. Not only will Lila continue to learn from large knowledge bases, such as Wikipedia, but it will also learn from people like you. Every question and answer given to Lila will be fed back into Lila's learning algorithm. Every conversation with Lila will make Lila a better tutor, in turn making Lila's students better learners.
